# S04: Extract Agumon presentation into its own module

**Goal:** Extract every Agumon-specific datum and control-flow out of the windowed engine files (src/windowed/mod.rs, src/windowed/render.rs) into a new src/windowed/digimon/agumon/ module that registers all of it through a register(app) entry point. The engine systems stay (they are generic players/state-machines) but are re-pointed to read from generic registries instead of AGUMON_* consts and closed match statements. After this: in cargo winx Agumon behaves exactly as before, but grep of the engine files shows no AGUMON_* const, no closed on_enter_effect_ids match, and no load_agumon_enoki_vfx — all of it lives in and is registered by src/windowed/digimon/agumon/. Windowed build/test green; the dep-gating test still proves no enoki/windowed leak into headless.

- 1) src/windowed/render.rs and src/windowed/mod.rs contain zero `AGUMON_` consts, no `fn on_enter_effect_ids`, no `fn skill_start_node`, no `fn load_agumon_enoki_vfx`, and no `effect_path`/`enoki_effect_path` match. 2) The five generic registries (EnokiVfxRegistry, OnEnterEffectRegistry, SkillStartNodeRegistry, SkillReleaseEffectRegistry, SpritePresentationRegistry) plus DetonateEffectRegistry are defined as engine resources, init-empty in the plugins, and populated only by src/windowed/digimon/agumon/. 3) cargo build --features windowed green with zero warnings. 4) cargo test --features windowed --test windowed_only green (including the new agumon_module_extraction contract test). 5) cargo test --test dependency_gating green (2/2; no enoki leak into headless).
