# S01: Bind the atlas: idle stance + basic render on both actors

**Goal:** Bind the existing 512px Agumon grid atlas to the on-screen Bevy Sprite so both the Agumon ally and the mirrored Agumon dummy render real pixels: idle stance loops and the basic Sharp Claws animation plays with damage landing on the rendered impact frame. Establish a Bevy-free atlas-geometry seam in the lib (AtlasGeometry::from_clip_meta + identity frame→atlas-index map) that headless tests exercise, then build the real TextureAtlasLayout windowed-side and drive Sprite.texture_atlas.index from the AnimGraphPlayer's current frame each tick.
**Demo:** Headless: tests prove the on-screen Sprite carries a bound Handle<Image>+TextureAtlas (not ..default()), the player frame maps 1:1 onto TextureAtlas.index across idle/attack ranges, clip↔atlas parity holds, and the impact-frame damage invariant holds on the rendered frame for Sharp Claws. Visual (user-run cargo winx, K001): two Agumon idle-loop and the basic Sharp Claws animation plays with damage landing on the impact frame.

## Must-Haves

- 1) Lib: AtlasGeometry::from_clip_meta reproduces agumon ClipMeta (frame_size 512x512, columns 10, rows 10, total_frames 93) and atlas_index is an identity map returning Some(frame) for frame<93 and None for frame>=93. 2) Headless (tests/animation harness): AnimGraphPlayer frames across the idle stance range (clip 53-58) and the Sharp Claws windup->strike nodes map 1:1 onto valid atlas indices within their clip ranges; the Sharp Claws ReleaseKernel cue's local frame maps through atlas_index to the rendered atlas frame inside the attack range (impact-frame-on-rendered-frame invariant). 3) Windowed: spawn_unit_sprites binds a real Handle<Image> + TextureAtlas (TextureAtlasLayout built from ClipMeta, not ..default()) and advance_agumon_presentation drives texture_atlas.index from the player frame; cargo build --features windowed is green and the full headless suite stays green. 4) Visual (user-run cargo winx, K001): two Agumon idle-loop and Sharp Claws plays with damage on the impact frame.
